You said yes. You survived the planning, the seating chart debates, and the cake tastings. Now it's time for the two of you to disappear to a beach somewhere beautiful. But with so many gorgeous coastlines calling your name, choosing the best beach destinations for your honeymoon can feel surprisingly overwhelming. This guide ranks 15 spots through the lens of what actually matters to newlyweds: romance, privacy, ease of travel after an exhausting wedding week, and whether the destination can pull double duty as a [destination wedding](/destination-wedding-guide/) location too.

Whether you're still admiring that ring from [Blue Nile](https://www.bluenile.com/){rel="noopener noreferrer sponsored"} or [Brilliant Earth](https://www.brilliantearth.com/){rel="noopener noreferrer sponsored"} or you're already deep into honeymoon research, we've got honest budget ranges, best-for labels, and real data to help you pick the right beach for your love story.

## What Makes a Beach Destination Truly Honeymoon-Worthy?

A great honeymoon beach offers more than pretty water and soft sand. It delivers privacy, romance-specific amenities, and the kind of ease that lets you actually relax after months of wedding planning.

According to [Honeymoons.com](https://honeymoons.com/honeymoon-stats-and-facts/), 99% of couples take a honeymoon after their wedding, and beaches remain the most popular choice. But not every beach is built for newlyweds. The best honeymoon beaches share a few qualities: adults-only or couples-focused resorts, natural beauty that feels intimate rather than crowded, activities you can enjoy together, and enough infrastructure that getting there doesn't require a PhD in logistics.

We also looked at whether each destination works as a combined wedding-and-honeymoon spot. If you're curious about that approach, our [destination wedding cost breakdown](/destination-wedding-cost/) covers the financial side in detail.

## How Do You Choose the Right Beach Honeymoon Destination for You?

Start with three honest questions: what's your budget, how far are you willing to fly, and what kind of experience excites you both?

According to [LuggageHero](https://luggagehero.com/blog/honeymoon-destinations-statistics/), 15% of U.S. honeymooners choose Mexico, 12% pick Hawaii, and 7% head to Jamaica. Those numbers reflect a preference for shorter flights and all-inclusive simplicity. But if you're willing to travel farther, destinations like Bali and Koh Lanta offer extraordinary value.

## The 15 Best Beach Destinations for Your Honeymoon

Each destination below gets a "best for" label so you can skip straight to the spots that match your vibe. No wading through irrelevant options.

## Maldives: The Overwater Bungalow Dream

**Best for: couples who want total seclusion and once-in-a-lifetime luxury.**

The [Maldives](/destinations/maldives/) is the gold standard for overwater romance. According to [Go Ocean Travel](https://gooceantravel.com/overwater-villas-in-the-maldives/), entry-level luxury villas start at $800 to $1,200 per night, with ultra-luxury options exceeding $3,000. The St. Regis Maldives Vommuli Resort offers private sandbank picnics and dedicated butler service, while Soneva Jani features private water slides into the lagoon and retractable roofs for stargazing.

One thing to know: seaplane transfers to outer atolls cost around $458 per person return, according to [Never Ending Voyage](https://www.neverendingvoyage.com/affordable-overwater-bungalow-maldives-reethi-beach/). Factor that into your budget early. **Combine with your wedding?** Absolutely. Many resorts offer intimate ceremony packages right on the water.

## Bali, Indonesia: Romance on a Budget (That Doesn't Feel Like It)

**Best for: couples who want luxury experiences without luxury prices.**

A 10-day [Bali](/destinations/bali/) honeymoon typically costs $4,800 to $9,800 including flights, according to [The Island Houses](https://theislandhouses.com/a-complete-budget-breakdown-for-a-10-day-honeymoon-in-bali/). Private pool villas run $200 to $450 per night, and a couple's massage costs $40 to $120. Daily food for two averages $60 to $90, with local warungs serving meals under $10.

Skip the July-August crowds. January through March offers better deals and surprising seclusion, contrary to what most people assume about off-season weather.

## Santorini, Greece: Sunsets That Will Ruin You for All Other Sunsets

**Best for: couples who want romance, culture, and jaw-dropping views.**

[Santorini](/destinations/santorini/) sits 120 miles southeast of mainland Greece, and its caldera sunsets from Oia draw thousands of visitors daily, according to [Travelers Joy](https://www.travelersjoy.com/blog/2022/11/10-reasons-why-santorini-is-the-best-honeymoon-destination.html). For a quieter experience, book a cliffside suite in Imerovigli, known as the "balcony of the Aegean."

Shoulder seasons (May or September) bring comfortable highs in the 70s°F and far fewer crowds than summer. Among [TripAdvisor's 19 Santorini honeymoon resorts](https://www.tripadvisor.com/HotelsList-Santorini-Honeymoon-Resorts-zfp14004.html), Imerovigli properties consistently rank highest for caldera views and intimacy.

## Hawaii: The Classic That Never Gets Old

**Best for: couples who want variety without a passport.**

According to [U.S. News & World Report](https://roadgenius.com/statistics/tourism/usa/hawaii/), Maui ranks as the number one island in [Hawaii](/destinations/hawaii/), with Wailea Beach serving as the most popular honeymoon beach. But don't overlook Lanai, which hosts only 3,000 residents and a single Four Seasons resort, according to [Andie Swim](https://andieswim.com/blogs/ooo-messages/best-beach-honeymoon-destinations).

Kailua Beach on Oahu stretches 2.5 miles with soft sand and aquamarine water, and it's far less crowded than Waikiki, per [Lonely Planet](https://www.lonelyplanet.com/articles/best-beaches-hawaii). A week in Hawaii averages $5,000 to $12,700.

## St. Lucia: The Most Underrated Romantic Island in the Caribbean

**Best for: couples who want Caribbean beauty with mountain drama.**

[St. Lucia](/destinations/st-lucia/) has won the World's Leading Honeymoon Destination title 16 times at the World Travel Awards, including seven consecutive wins from 2018 through 2024, according to the [Saint Lucia Tourism Authority](https://www.stlucia.org/en/press/saint-lucia-crowned-worlds-leading-honeymoon-destination-2024/). The UNESCO-listed Pitons rising from the sea create a backdrop unlike anything else in the Caribbean.

Stick to the Caribbean (west) side of the island for calm swimming waters. The Atlantic side is rougher and less suited for beach days.

## Turks and Caicos: The Best Beach Water You'll Ever See

**Best for: couples obsessed with water clarity and white sand.**

Grace Bay Beach spans 3 miles of powder-soft white sand protected by a barrier reef about one mile offshore, according to [World's 50 Best Beaches](https://worlds50beaches.com/grace-bay/). It ranked number 10 in the world for 2025. The sand stays cool underfoot due to its unique coral grain composition, per the [Turks & Caicos Tourism Board](https://www.visittci.com/nature-and-history/geology-ecology-climate/why-turks-and-caicos-has-the-best-beaches).

Photos aren't edited. The water really looks like that. [Turks and Caicos](/destinations/turks-and-caicos/) delivers exactly what you see online, and often more.

## Amalfi Coast, Italy: For Couples Who Want More Than Just Sand

**Best for: foodies, culture lovers, and couples who want cliffside romance.**

The [Amalfi Coast](/destinations/amalfi-coast/) spans 34 miles of dramatic cliffs and coastal towns, according to [Wildjoy Blog](https://blog.wildjoy.com/honeymoon-in-the-amalfi-coast/). Beaches here are mostly pebble coves, not sandy stretches. The magic is in the lemon groves, the pasta, and the views from your terrace in Ravello or Positano.

Plan 5 to 7 days and visit in May or September. According to [Italy Charme](https://www.italycharme.com/blog/amalfi-coast-honeymoon-planning-guide/), summer crowds and 90°F heat make July and August far less enjoyable.

## Tulum, Mexico: Bohemian Luxury Meets Ancient Magic

**Best for: wellness-focused couples who love history and boho vibes.**

[Tulum](/destinations/tulum/) holds Mexico's official Pueblo Mágico designation, recognizing its blend of Mayan ruins, cenotes, and beach culture, according to [A Passion and a Passport](https://apassionandapassport.com/tulum-itinerary-mexico/). Gran Cenote earns a 4.2 out of 5 from over 4,300 TripAdvisor reviews, and swimming in a sacred sinkhole together is about as romantic as it gets.

The beach strip is pricier and more touristy than the inland town. Budget-conscious couples can base themselves in town and taxi to the coast.

## Fiji: South Pacific Romance at Its Most Untouched

**Best for: adventurous couples seeking seclusion and marine life.**

[Fiji](/destinations/fiji/) comprises over 330 islands with only 110 permanently inhabited, according to [Turtle Island Fiji](https://www.turtlefiji.com/everything-you-need-to-know-about-a-fiji-honeymoon/). Turtle Island accommodates just 14 couples at a time on a private island. Fiji is recognized as the soft coral capital of the world, per [Namale Fiji](https://www.namalefiji.com/resources/5-reasons-why-fiji-is-the-ultimate-honeymoon-destination/), making it a top-tier snorkeling and diving destination.

Visit May through October for dry season temperatures around 75 to 85°F.

## Jamaica: All-Inclusive Done Right

**Best for: couples who want everything handled and nothing to stress about.**

[Jamaica](/destinations/jamaica/) pioneered the all-inclusive model over 30 years ago, according to [Unforgettable Honeymoons](https://www.unforgettablehoneymoons.com/honeymoon-destinations/caribbean/jamaica/why-travel-here/). The island offers 11 couples-only resorts and 12 adults-only properties, per [Honeymoons Inc](https://www.honeymoonsinc.com/resorts/location/caribbean-honeymoon/jamaica-honeymoon/). [Sandals](https://www.sandals.com/){rel="noopener noreferrer sponsored"} operates 7 properties here, with packages starting at $1,024 per person for 5 nights.

The Caves Negril features just 11 private cottages for an ultra-intimate cliffside experience. This isn't the crowded resort stereotype.

## Los Cabos, Mexico: Desert Meets Ocean in the Most Dramatic Way

**Best for: couples who want sunshine guaranteed and dramatic landscapes.**

[Los Cabos](/destinations/los-cabos/) delivers 300 to 350 sunny days per year, according to [Honeymoons Inc](https://www.honeymoonsinc.com/resorts/location/mexico-honeymoon/los-cabos-honeymoon). The desert-meets-ocean scenery around El Arco is unlike any other beach destination. Important note: many beaches here have dangerous rip currents. Stick to swimmable spots like Medano Beach, Chileno Bay, and Santa Maria Bay.

## Punta Cana: The Easiest Yes in the Caribbean

**Best for: first-time international travelers who want zero hassle.**

[Punta Cana](/destinations/punta-cana/) maintains average temperatures between 81 and 88°F year-round, according to [Lopesan Costa Bavaro](https://www.lopesancostabavaro.com/en/blog/weddings-honeymoon/reasons-go-punta-cana-honeymoon/). Secrets Cap Cana tops TripAdvisor's honeymoon list with over 25,500 reviews and a 4.9-star rating. Short flights from the U.S. East Coast (3 to 4 hours) make this the lowest-effort Caribbean escape.

## Aruba: The Sunny Escape That Almost Never Rains

**Best for: couples who refuse to gamble on weather.**

[Aruba](/destinations/aruba/) sits outside the hurricane belt and maintains an average temperature of 82°F year-round with cooling trade winds, according to [Love at First Travel](https://loveatfirsttravel.com/aruba-one-happy-honeymoon-some-budget-tips/). Eagle Beach ranks number 7 in the world, and Aruba has the highest return visitor rate of any Caribbean island, with over half of visitors coming back.

Bucuti & Tara Beach Resort, a couples-only property, holds a 4.9 out of 5 TripAdvisor rating and was once named the most romantic hotel in the world.

## Algarve, Portugal: Europe's Most Stunning (and Affordable) Coastline

**Best for: couples who want European charm without European prices.**

The [Algarve](/destinations/algarve/) stretches over 200km with more than 100 beaches, according to [Live Like It's the Weekend](https://www.livelikeitstheweekend.com/best-beaches-algarve-portugal/). Praia da Falésia runs over 6km and was voted one of the best beaches in the world by TripAdvisor in 2024. Vila Vita Parc earns a 4.8 out of 5 rating, and you'll spend significantly less than you would on the Amalfi Coast or Santorini.

## Koh Lanta, Thailand: For Couples Who Want to Disappear Together

**Best for: couples craving quiet, affordability, and authentic Thai culture.**

[Koh Lanta](/destinations/koh-lanta/) stretches nearly 20 miles with 11 white sand beaches, according to [RTW in 30 Days](https://rtwin30days.com/thailands-best-islands-koh-lanta/). Long Beach runs nearly two miles and is described as "practically empty" compared to most Thai beaches. Kantiang Bay in the south caters specifically to honeymooners seeking luxury and seclusion.

This island feels less like a party destination and more like a place to truly slow down together.

## Should You Combine Your Destination Wedding and Honeymoon?

Yes, and it can save you both money and vacation days. Many resorts build packages specifically for this.

According to [The Wedding Edition](https://www.theweddingedition.co.uk/planning-a-destination-wedding-and-honeymoon-here-are-the-top-destinations-to-say-i-do/), Grenada's Spice Island Beach Resort provides complimentary weddings for selected room categories, including the ceremony, marriage certificate, floral decorations, and a sunset cruise. Jamaica's Couples Swept Away includes bouquets, sparkling wine, and catamaran cruises in their wedding packages.

Our [destination wedding tips](/destination-wedding-tips/) article covers the logistics of making this work. If you're considering it, start planning 6 to 12 months out and confirm legal requirements with the local embassy or a [destination wedding planner](/vendors/), since rules change frequently.

## What Does a Beach Honeymoon Actually Cost?

The average U.S. couple spends $5,300 to $6,500 on their honeymoon, depending on the source. According to [The Knot](https://www.theknot.com/content/average-cost-of-honeymoon), the 2024 average was $5,300, while [Honeyfund](https://www.honeyfund.com/content/weddingmoon/weddingmoon-cost) projects $6,500 for 2026.

According to [Jetset Christina](https://www.jetsetchristina.com/how-to-choose-a-honeymoon-destination-by-budget-what-you-can-expect-to-spend-on-your-honeymoon-for-the-top-10-honeymoon-destinations/), Hawaii averages $5,000 to $12,700 for a week, while the Maldives can run $11,000 to $40,000 or more. Bora Bora's food and drink alone hits $3,500 to $4,500 per week due to resort pricing.

The smartest move? Set your total budget first, then pick your destination. Our [destination wedding cost guide](/destination-wedding-cost/) can help you think through the numbers if you're combining events. And always keep 10% of your budget as a buffer for surprises.
