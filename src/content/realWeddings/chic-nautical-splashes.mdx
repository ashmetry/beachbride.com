In this social media driven world, there are countless chances that you will find your one true love. Yet, for Moni and Chris’ case, they did. And this is why we are here: To see how their life together officially unfolded. Their style, chic and nautical, darlings! And that is such a howling and beautiful choice for their fierce love. Keep scrolling to find out more fun! THE WEDDING DETAILS Chic and Nautical The flowing pastel dresses of the bridesmaids were sure winners as were the navy contrasts. To add more body on their scheme, they let gold do the job. And as the sun fell to sleep, fairy lights took charge of lighting the entire place. Keep the Love Burning They hired a fire dancer, people! That is insanely burning, literally and figuratively. Ha-ha! This is one fun Costa Rican inspired wedding party and I would love to be in it (If I could)! Splish Splash I don’t know how they ended up in the pool with their dresses on but everyone’s faces looked like they enjoyed a whole lot! With cocktails on their hands and booming party music, who would ever want to end a day like this? Congratulations, you two! Enjoy your life together.
