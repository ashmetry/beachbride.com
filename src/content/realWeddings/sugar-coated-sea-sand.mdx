Wine glasses clink as IL and JL spent one fine day in the beach, having picnic and enjoying each other’s company. And since this is one save-the-date announcement for their big day, they incorporated their wedding colors in the session -- hot pink and teal. You may feel like you’re taking some screenshot peeks from a movie but no, this is an engagement shoot captured passionately by Havana Photography. This couple is a natural! Congratulations IL and JL! This couple contacted me for pictures back in the fall. They are getting married in an island this year and wanted to incorporate their wedding colors hot pink and teal plus the beach. They wanted to follow a cohesive theme with their destination wedding and although we toughed out the cold and wind, we accomplish a successful beach theme engagement session. The couple love drinking wine so we had a picnic on the beach as well. It was super beautiful and we closed our session with a beautiful sunset. VENDOR Photographer: Havana Photography
