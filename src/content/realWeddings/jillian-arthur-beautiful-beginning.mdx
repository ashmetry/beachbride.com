Engagement sessions always has a way of capturing our hearts & cause us to go in a mild series of daydreams.  This beautiful casual engagement shoot by POPography was done at the lovely Atlantic City.  It was where Jillian & Arthur first met.

So this place holds a special spot in the couple's hearts. It was the perfect time to capture the memories of where it all began.  Even with the sudden possibility of severe thunderstorms that day, it didn't hinder Jillian & Arthur to have such a fun & perfect day.

These images would speak for themselves.  Cheers again to Jillian & Arthur!
