We barely got a beach shot from this wedding but we definitely don't mind because that Miami skyline is just amazing.  It just goes to show how Miami perfected the mixture of "urban lifestyle" and "beach relaxation".  Just like how this wedding smoothly combined formal and fun from the looks of the ceremony and the reception.

Look out for the dance floor pictures where you can almost hear the music's beat and feel the rave vibe from their guest.  But what you really shouldn't miss from this feature is how how the photographer captured our groom's emotions when his bride walked down the aisle - that shot is truly incomparable.  Congratulations to our couple, Bianca & Alex!

Photographer: Bianca and Alex's elegant wedding took place at The Rusty Pelican, a historic Miami restaurant overlooking Biscayne Bay in Miami, Florida.  Bianca chose a modern, chic color palette of ivory and white with hints of silver.  Extremely elegant and pretty!

Our two top wedding photographers worked together to capture this gorgeous event and we're so pleased with the results.  The lighting was especially lovely for a Summer wedding in South Florida!  And of course the view of the Miami skyline after the sun sets is stunning from the floor-to-ceiling windows of The Rusty Pelican's ballroom.
