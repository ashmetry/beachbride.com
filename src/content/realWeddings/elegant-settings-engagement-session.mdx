As much as we're in love with the sand and sea, it's nice to once in a while deviate from what we're used to - just to add some variety to our palette.  But of course, the day we choose to do this, we must present you guys something that's noteworthy and we promise that you won't be disappointed.  Our couple today, Nina & Vincent, chose elegant locations on their engagement shoot.

From a beautiful park, to the inside and outside of a lovely castle-like structure, they really knew how to mix up style and venues.  Congratulations on an awesome engagement shoot you two!  Photographer I met Vincent and Nina about 1 year ago at our old studio location.

Immediately upon our initial meet we started speaking about the wedding and all of the different plans we had to make sure all of the important moments were covered.  My initial reaction when meeting them was , " they really do love each other, it works ".  Vincent very calm and charming self, and Nina, smiley faces and charismatic ways around Vinny .

They had an amazing great sense of style and the location that they choose was original.  Nina actually is very familiar with the Mill Neck Mansion having visit the site campus they decided this was a great fit for the theme and look that they were looking for .  It was great seeing them work the camera.

It was very natural with very minimal direction.  The photographs speak for themselves.
