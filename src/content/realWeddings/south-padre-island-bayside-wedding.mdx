It was a day of never-ending fun as Nadia and Damian celebrated their wedding day with a big crowd of fa mily and friends at one of the beaches of South Padre Island in Texas! The elegant o ceanside ceremony held at Cameron Country Amphitheater featured white floral structures adorning the altar by the beach where the groom excitedly awaited to see his gorgeous bride walking down the aisle to the modern instrumental music. As the ceremony ended, guests strolled towards an open bar and charcuterie cups on the lawn awaiting the bridal party as they posed for memories made to last a lifetime by Anette Miguel Photography & Videography . LongBoard Bar and Grill , a bayside beach bar , was completely transformed into a luxurious and romantic setting overlooking the sunset at the bay waters. Floral chandeliers adorned the outdoor canopies while rows of lush white blooms ornamented the luxurious tablescapes - such an amazing sight by Cantu’s Special Events . Setting the mood was a sweet melody of the string quartet , Viol Consort , as guests found their seats to watch the sunset along the bay. A Grande Soiree Events LLC poured in entertainment to make the party alive all night! Guests enjoyed an open bar, a surf and turf dinner by the Queen’s Kitchen , a custom coffee cart and a late-night taquiza ! There was never a boring moment with the string quartet serenading the couple during dinner, a live band, a club style DJ, photo booths and an ear piercing station! A generous display of w hite blossoms surrounded the elegant three-tier cake by Honey’s cakes . The guests were delighted as the couple provided individually packa ged dessert boxes to take home for them to indulge in . It was really an unforgettable day as t he night ended with a fireworks display over Laguna Madre Bay and a mariachi serenade.
