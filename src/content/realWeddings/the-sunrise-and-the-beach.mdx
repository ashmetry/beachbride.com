If you love sunrise and beaches then this e-session is definitely for you.  The feeling you get when you see the sun rising is the same as getting engaged - welcoming the new day, new challenges, and new life.  It's basically the things you look forward, coming in the present.

And, above all, the romantic setting will take it up a notch.  What inspired your outfits for the engagement session?  I always suggest for couples to wear flowing dresses at the beach if possible.

They look feminine and soft, especially at sunrise.  Do the photos give a hint on what will happen on their wedding day?  How so?

Not really.  This couple will be married at Bella Collina in Clermont.  It has more of an Italian atmosphere.

How did you tell the couple to feel at ease with all the posed shootings?  I reminded them to look into each other's eyes.  To talk to each other and to connect.

Touching, hand holding, and embracing one another always photographs well!
