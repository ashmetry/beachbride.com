A passionate wedding on the beach with the man I love and all the special persons in my life is what I have always dreamed of.  And oohh, today’s feature carried me away!  It’s just that Lori and Ross had a wonderful wedding day.

It was an intimate day with the people they love at Huracan Cafe .  It’s rustic beachy feel, unique and dazzling decorations plus the delicious food and the starlit dance floor were all amazing.  This is so great and I say congratulations to both of you!

Katya Nova Photography is sharing to us their picture lovely moments at the wedding, big thanks!

**Extra Special Details**

The brother of the bride officiated the wedding ceremony.  The sparkler going off and everyone jumping back.  They had their first dance and they had it with a sign “Better Together”.

The “Crazy Hour” by HOTS Productions which is a Dominican tradition that at first made the guests shocked but it was really fun and they happily enjoyed it.  The Bride’s Advice ♥ Do not worry about the small details.  When I think about our wedding, I really only think about the fun night that we had, and looking back, I probably would have cut some costs on some of the decor and details.

Enjoy the planning!  Do not let this time stress you out, because this time is can be so fun and goes by so fast!  Spend the money on a good photographer.

We did, and am so happy because of our photos, by Katya Nova, are absolutely stunning and we still can't stop looking through all of them!  They were worth every penny and the kindest people!  We also did a "Trash the Dress" session with them, which was very fun!

Our photo booth was also something fun for our guests but we love the pictures to look back on too.  I do regret not hiring a videographer.  Would be nice to have a video to shed a tear too!
