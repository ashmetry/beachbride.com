Let this styled shoot inspire and take you to the paradise otherwise known as Hawaii.  This beautiful island is known for it's laidback and relaxing vibe - they don't call it vacation island for nothing.  So if you're having your special day here or even if you just want to capture the essence of it, then let this simple styled shoot be your guide.

The DIY floral details and dress are perfect for beach and mellow weddings!  Photographer I'm in love with the islands, even though I have never been.  I needed an outlet during a crazy wedding season to just be creative and thus: The Aloha Series.

100% DIY from the floral crown to the dress.  A simple favor to the local VW Bus Club and we were set for a styled shoot.  The most gorgeous sunlight I have ever witnessed covered First Landing State Park for this shoot.
