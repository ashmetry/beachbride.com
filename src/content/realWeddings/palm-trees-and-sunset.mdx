Soft sand, cool ocean breeze, salty air - all these make for a beautiful beach wedding.  Add some palm trees and gorgeous sunset, now you have the best day ever!  Erin and Nick's big day became more special of the palm trees.

These two had been dating since high school, and they got married shortly before they hit that 10 years of dating mark.  They're all grown up and absolutely ready for a new life together!

**What Made The Day**

Extra Special Palm trees!  Who doesn't love palm tress?  It seems people who live in New England (where winters are cold and snowy), have an appreciation for palms trees and other tropical plants even more than maybe the people who live among them.

This made the day extra special!

**The Photographer**

's Advice Know the sunset time on your wedding date.  Make sure your photographer knows that you'll want to get some gorgeous sky images!  Be ready to be flexible.

I knew they wanted those images.  Because things were running later than planned, as soon as the toasts were complete, we had to race right out there.  Racing the sun is always a possibility.
