Tommy ♥ Vanessa As an operator of an event furniture and prop rental company, Vanessa surely achieved her boho-chic wedding quite effortlessly.  This beautiful event is indeed a wedding made of bohemian dreams -- from the invites up to the lovely adornments.  The Sun & Surf Beach Club in Long Beach, NY was a perfect oasis for the boho-chic theme they wanted.

The boathouse-style raw space was the perfect canvas for them to create their own aesthetic and theme.  Browse their wedding gallery for you might get an idea or two!

**Extra Special Details**

The bridal style.  To achieve her hippie chic vibe Vanessa wore a subtle mermaid dress with her hair down (simple but oh so chic) topped off with a flower crown to get more bohemian.  And yes, there are a lot of substitutes for the traditional veil.

The theme.  Bohemian / Hippie Chic The décor.  The décor was stylishly done.

Vanessa and her team created a beautiful oasis for their bohemian dreams.  From the flowers dangling around the venue to the hippiest and chicest furniture you can see.  The colour palette.

Ivory, navy, and pink.  The flowers.  It was a mix of deep reds, blush pinks, white hydrangeas and yellow accents.

The centerpieces.  Gold bases holding a wide variety of white and blush florals to compliment the gold chandeliers above the dance floor.
