Let's start Sunday with Teresa & Tom's intimate beach wedding ceremony. Check out their vibrant blues & coral hues theme as it blends perfectly with the white sands of Hilton Clearwater Beach Resort . I loved how the color blue pops out of the coral crowd in the bouquet, beautifully & creatively arranged by Iza's Flowers for the bride & bridesmaids. The bride also placed starfish accessories on her hair, which was the perfect addition for a beach wedding. Don't you just love how the mixture of turquoise & coral can pull off the perfect beach wedding theme? The turquoise color gives you that cool & relaxing feeling of being in water. Its gorgeous vibrance would instantly uplift the mood for such a big, happy day! Teresa actually handcrafted some of the decors on her wedding. She bought a lantern from Home Goods, decorated it with ribbon & starfish instead of using a traditional card box. She also handpainted a wish box for each guest to write their wish for the bride & groom. They also purchased a variety of favors, placed them inside different pouches & put a ribbon as finish touch. They did their seating chart by stringing hemp across a frame & attaching escort cards with miniature clothes pins. Overall, this wedding was truly beautiful & memorable! Congratulations again, Teresa & Tom!
