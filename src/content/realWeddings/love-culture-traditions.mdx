Vian ♥ AJ Vian and AJ did not have one but two ceremonies!  The couple combined the and Iranian and Hindu wedding traditions into one big fun and romantic wedding.  I'm very impressed of how planned everything.

This upbeat and hyper wedding celebration started from 11 am and ended 11 pm!  It was truly and eventful day for Vian and AJ.

**Extra Special Details**

The bridal style.  For the Hindu ceremony, Vian wore a traditional Lehenga with beautiful accessories all over.  The decor.

This culture packed wedding had on point decorations!  The colorful curtains surrounding the couple as they perform Hindu rituals.  Two ceremonies.

As I have mentioned earlier the couple is from two different cultures and decided to combine the to get the best of both worlds!  Tips For Brides-to-Be!  We always suggest to couples that are doing a "symbolic" ceremony...

that you make your destination wedding where you actually say your vows and get married your first time.  You can always do the legal aspect of the union when you get home.  The emotion you feel the first time you get married together, legal or symbolic, is always fresh and amazing.

Tears will be shed so please make this the time when you are going to celebrate with friends and family!
