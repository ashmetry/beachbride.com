This couple decided to do a traditional wedding in an untraditional way.  They chose their attire to look formal with a bit of fun touches to it like using coral colored dresses for the bridesmaids & turquoise colored ties, socks & shoe strings for the groomsmen.  They also decided not to see each until the day of the wedding.

The first look was the bride's personal favorite moment on her big day.  Since their destination wedding was held in Mexico, Sarah thought it would be cute to have their terms of endearment engraved on the rings in Spanish (Mi Amor) which she presented to Eric at the wedding.  And having the priest ask for a selfie with them after the ceremony at the church was pretty priceless!

Sarah pulled another surprise for Eric when she had fireworks go off during their first dance.  Such a sweet bride, right?  It is always this little personal touches that makes a wedding truly special.

So cheers to such a beautiful & loving couple!
