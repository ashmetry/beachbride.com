The Sunset Ballroom at Jaker’s Point , New Jersey was really a perfect beach setting for weddings and gladly, Mr. & Mrs. Michael ideally picked this beautiful place. It highly complemented the couple’s gray and blue color theme and everything fell so magically. Love shined so bright throughout the celebration as will be seen in all the lovely photographs taken by Enchanted Celebrations . Take a peek guys and give your sweet smile of happiness! Extra Special Details The first look with a few drops of rain. The ceremony was held on the bride’s grandfather’s birthday who passed three years ago as a way of honor for him. The officiant was their good friend and it happened to be his first wedding. Wow! The venue of their wedding was the beach they travel to in the winter with their dogs and to look for sea glass. Tips from the Bride “Just relax. The time moves so fast leading up to the event and the day of. It is such a rewarding experience and there is no need to stress...about anything. Everything falls into place.” Money Matters Wedding Budget: $20,000 - $45,000 Vendors Photographer: Enchanted Celebrations Reception Venue: Jack Baker's Sunset Ballroom
