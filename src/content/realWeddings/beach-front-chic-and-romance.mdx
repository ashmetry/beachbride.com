The classic beachfront wedding is always a must when you plan on a beach setting.  Michael and Krystle managed to pull off that romantic wedding in front of the beach.  Everything was chic and fun with green and blue as their motif.

I was amazed as I went through their wedding photos that were shot by their great photographer, Karma Hill.  Congratulations on this journey Michael and Krystle!  Album Story: Michael and Krystle had a romantic and classic wedding ocean front in Maui at Sugar Beach Events Hale.

Although it was a destination wedding, they lots of friends and family there with them to help them celebrate.

**Favorite Part of the Day**

: The ceremony was so heartfelt and beautiful.  The fact that the pacific ocean was the backdrop made it even more special!  The Do-It-Yourself (DIY) Projects: No DIY but they did have cute details such as Mr.

Right and Mrs.  Always right on the bride and groom's chairs, beautiful custom made invitations in royal blue and green, and a funny custom cake topper of them sitting in the sand together.  The Highlights From The Ceremony & Reception: The reception included entertainment by the couple's guests, a full hip hop dance number was choreographed and performed.

It added a special touch to the evening entertainment!

**The Theme**

: Fun and Dancing!! Green and Blue Most Memorable Moment: I am sure the family dance number is something they will never forget!
