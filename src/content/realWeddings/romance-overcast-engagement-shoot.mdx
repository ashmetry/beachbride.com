An overcast day can be such a downer for some people, specially if you're having your engagement photos taken that day. But Andrea and Mitchell turned things around and nailed this shoot with their romantic poses and sweet smiles. If their photographer didn't point it out, we wouldn't even have noticed the weather because we're so focused on the bright vibes these two are giving us. She even hopped on the "positive" train and considered the cloud as some sort of light diffusion that created unique effects. Congratulations Andrea and Mitchell! Photographer: Florida weather can be unpredictable, and I had to reschedule with Andrea and Mitchell at least two times before we finally did their engagement photo-shoot. Every time we nailed a date, a strong thunderstorm always rolled in hours prior to our session, so we had to keep postponing to the days we were all available. They wanted a sunset shoot, but it had been raining every afternoon lately, so we finally opted for a sunrise shoot. Of course, with our luck, it rained prior to our session again and remained cloudy by the time we started--but I could not ask for a better and perfect overcast morning for our photo-shoot! The clouds were amazing and provided us with the greatest diffused light. We could see the sunshine on the horizon, and the lighting was enough to give Andrea and Mitchell a warm glow on their skin even if we had gloomy skies.
